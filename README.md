# 多模态 AI 认知康复训练系统

# **Multimodal AI Cognitive Rehabilitation System**

利用多模态 AI 个性化生成模型，为认知障碍患者（尤其是阿尔茨海默病患者）提供高度定制化的图像、视频、音频内容，显著提升参与度与康复效果。

Leveraging multimodal personalized AI generation models, this system provides highly customized visual, video, and audio content for individuals with cognitive impairment—especially Alzheimer’s disease—to greatly enhance engagement and rehabilitation outcomes.

---

# Part 1：多维度认知康复训练刺激内容生成

# **Part 1: Multidimensional Cognitive Training Stimulus Generation**

## 1. 情景回忆内容生成

### **1. Scene-Based Autobiographical Memory Generation**

基于患者少量照片 + ID 一致的个性化模型，生成患者在“熟悉场景”中的高保真图像（如购物、做饭、看书、散步等），精准激发自传式记忆。

Using a small number of patient photos and an identity-consistent personalized model, the system generates high-fidelity images of the patient in **familiar daily-life scenes** (shopping, cooking, reading, walking, etc.), effectively triggering autobiographical memory.

<img src="./image/sence.png" width="650"/>

---

## 2. 年龄定制化照片生成

### **2. Age-Progression / Regression Photo Generation**

一键生成患者**少年、青年、中年、老年**四个阶段的照片，辅助回忆人生轨迹与亲友关系。

With one click, the system generates the patient’s images across **four life stages**—childhood, youth, midlife, and older age—to support structured life-story recall.

<img src="./image/age.png" width="650"/>

---

## 3. “找不同”注意力训练素材批量生成

### **3. Batch Generation of “Spot the Difference” Attention Training Materials**

自动生成大量专业级“找不同”图片组，用于训练注意力集中、视觉搜索与视空间能力。

Automatically produces dozens of professional “spot-the-difference” image pairs for attention, visual search, and visuospatial training.

<img src="./image/difference.png" width="650"/>

---

## 4. 多样化视觉刺激训练任务

### **4. Diverse Visual Cognitive Training Tasks**

同一套生成素材可灵活适配多类认知训练：

The same generated materials can flexibly support multiple cognitive domains:

* **记忆 Memory**
* **注意力 Attention**
* **视空间能力 Visuospatial Skills**
* **执行功能 Executive Function**
* **逻辑推理 Logical Reasoning**

<img src="./image/diversity.png" width="650"/>

---

## 5. 情感修复级内容生成

### **5. Emotion-Restorative Content Generation**

可生成患者“从未拍过的珍贵照片”：如年轻时的结婚照、与已故亲人的合影等，提供强烈的情感支持与回忆触发。

Generates emotionally meaningful “missing photographs,” such as wedding photos from youth or images with deceased loved ones, providing deep emotional comfort and memory activation.

<img src="./image/wedding.png" width="750"/>

# Part 2：VR 多感官认知与平衡康复训练

# **Part 2: VR Multisensory Cognitive & Balance Rehabilitation**

专为兼有认知与平衡障碍的患者设计，融合视觉空间、前庭觉、本体觉与感统处理的沉浸式 VR 训练。

Designed for patients with both cognitive and balance impairments, integrating visuospatial training, vestibular stimulation, proprioception, and sensory integration in immersive VR environments.

# Part 3：个性化音视频回忆故事生成

# **Part 3: Personalized Audio-Video Life-Story Generation**

## **核心输入（仅需三项）**

## **Required Inputs (Only Three Needed)**

* 患者及亲友的照片各 1 张
  One photo of the patient + one photo of a family member
* 1 段亲友的真实语音（用于声音克隆）
  A short voice sample for voice cloning
* 简短故事描述
  A short text description of the memory/story

## **系统自动生成**

## **System Output**

完整、高情感共鸣的 AI 回忆故事视频（含亲友声音旁白 + 患者形象）。

A complete, emotionally resonant AI-generated memory video featuring the patient, narrated in the family member’s cloned voice.
